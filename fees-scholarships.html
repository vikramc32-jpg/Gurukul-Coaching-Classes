Fees & Scholarships

Transparent fee structure and opportunities for financial assistance.

Fee Structure Overview

Our fee structure is designed to be transparent and competitive, reflecting the quality of education and resources we provide. Fees vary based on the course, duration, and batch type (online/offline). For detailed fee information, please contact our admission counselors.

Payment Options:

  • Full payment at the time of admission (often with a discount).
  • Installment plans (monthly/quarterly).
  • Online payment through our secure portal.

Scholarship Programs

Gurukul Coaching Classes is committed to supporting meritorious and deserving students. We offer various scholarship programs to help ease the financial burden and encourage academic excellence.

Types of Scholarships:

  • Merit-Based Scholarships: Awarded to students who perform exceptionally well in our scholarship test or have outstanding academic records.
  • Early Bird Discounts: Special discounts for students who enroll early in our programs.
  • Sibling Discounts: Concessions for families enrolling multiple children.
  • Financial Aid: Limited assistance for students from economically weaker sections, based on eligibility criteria.

How to Apply for Scholarships

To apply for a scholarship, you typically need to:

  1. Fill out the scholarship application form (available at our center or upon request).
  2. Appear for the Gurukul Scholarship Test (dates announced periodically).
  3. Submit relevant academic and financial documents (if applying for financial aid).

For more information on fees and scholarships, please feel free to contact us or visit our center.
