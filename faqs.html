Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about Gurukul Coaching Classes.

General Questions

Q: What courses does Gurukul Coaching Classes offer?

A: We offer comprehensive coaching for school academics (Class 1-12), and entrance exams like NEET, JEE, SSC, and Banking exams. Please visit our Courses page for more details.

Q: How can I apply for admission?

A: You can apply for admission through our online registration form available on the Admissions page. You can also visit our center for offline registration.

Q: Do you offer demo classes?

A: Yes, we offer free demo classes for most of our courses. Please contact us to schedule a demo class for your preferred course.

Q: What are the class timings?

A: Class timings vary by course and batch. We offer both morning and evening batches. Detailed schedules are provided upon admission.

Fees & Scholarships

Q: What are the fees for your courses?

A: Our fees vary depending on the course and duration. Please contact our admission counselors for detailed fee structures. You can find contact information on our Contact Us page.

Q: Do you offer any scholarships?

A: Yes, we offer scholarships based on merit and financial need. Students can appear for our scholarship test to avail discounts on fees. Details are available on the Admissions page.

Online Classes

Q: Are online classes available?

A: Yes, we provide interactive online classes for students who prefer to learn from home. Our online platform ensures a seamless learning experience with live sessions, doubt clearing, and digital study material.

Q: How do online classes work?

A: Our online classes are conducted via a dedicated virtual classroom platform. Students can join live sessions, interact with teachers, submit assignments, and access recorded lectures for revision.
