Online Classes

Our Online Learning Platform

Gurukul Coaching Classes brings quality education to your doorstep with our state-of-the-art online learning platform. Designed for flexibility and effectiveness, our online classes ensure you receive the same high standard of teaching and support as our offline programs.

Key Features:

  • Live Interactive Sessions: Engage with expert faculty in real-time, ask questions, and participate in discussions.
  • Recorded Lectures: Access recordings of all live classes for revision and to catch up on missed sessions.
  • Comprehensive Study Material: Downloadable notes, practice sheets, and reference materials.
  • Regular Assessments: Online tests and quizzes to track your progress and identify areas for improvement.
  • Dedicated Doubt Clearing: Get your queries resolved promptly through dedicated online doubt sessions and forums.
  • Flexible Scheduling: Choose from various batch timings to fit your schedule.
