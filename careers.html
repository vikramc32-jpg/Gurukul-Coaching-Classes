Careers at Gurukul

Join our team of dedicated educators and staff.

Why Work at Gurukul Coaching Classes?

At Gurukul, we believe in fostering a dynamic and supportive environment where educators can thrive and make a real difference in students' lives. We are committed to academic excellence, innovative teaching methodologies, and continuous professional development for our team.

Our Values:

  • Student-Centric Approach: Our students' success is at the heart of everything we do.
  • Excellence in Education: We strive for the highest standards in teaching and learning.
  • Innovation: We embrace new ideas and technologies to enhance the learning experience.
  • Integrity: We uphold strong ethical principles in all our interactions.
  • Teamwork: We believe in collaborative efforts to achieve common goals.

Current Opportunities

We are always looking for passionate, dedicated, and experienced individuals to join our growing family. If you are enthusiastic about shaping the future of young minds, we encourage you to explore our current openings:

Faculty Positions:

  • Physics Educator (JEE/NEET Foundation)
  • Chemistry Educator (JEE/NEET Foundation)
  • Mathematics Educator (School & Competitive)
  • Biology Educator (NEET)
  • English Language Trainer

Administrative & Support Staff:

  • Academic Counselor
  • Front Desk Executive
  • Marketing Executive

How to Apply

If you are interested in any of the positions listed above, or if you believe you can contribute to our mission in another capacity, please send your detailed resume and a cover letter to careers@gurukulclasses.com. Please mention the position you are applying for in the subject line.

We look forward to hearing from you!
